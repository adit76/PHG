Who are we?

Nepal being an agricultural country with ample of natural resource and biodiversity and high tourism possibility, development and sustainable use of resources is must for development. Making the optimal benefit of these resources, increasing the literacy and adopting the indispensable need of good governance, disregarding the political and community influence in the conservation and management of environment natural resources and biodiversity and their sustainability to support the economic status of country, Peopleâ€™s Help Group was established in Poush 4, 2070 B.S. Objectives

  • Improve the livelihood of rural community through natural resources conservation and their sustainable management.
  • Adopt participatory approach in resource conservation and management, and equity in benefit sharing to boost up local community livelihood through their sustainable use.
  • Empower for a united and self reliant local community
  • Conduct climate change environment conservation and awareness related programs.
  • Conduct study and research regarding natural resource, environment and biodiversity .
  • Address climate change adaptation and mitigation for Natural Resource Management.
  • Conduct skill developing and income generating program to alleviate poverty and support rural economy.
  • Coordination with different organizations and civil society to empower the development of rural communities.
  • Develop a unique tourism destination by use of local culture and natural resources to increase income of local people.
